WhaleUtils aims to simplify the long codes of different subjects by providing a variety of modules. The library supports Kotlin and Java for Regular Android Development and Jetpack Compose.

---

## Concepts

Here are some concepts and faithfulness of the library.

### Repetitiveness

It's hoped that the utility we provided, will be useful for every projects you made. By repeating using the utils, you can boost up your efficency.
